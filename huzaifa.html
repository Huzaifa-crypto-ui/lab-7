Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba

What is Demon Slayer?

Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba (Japanese: 鬼滅の刃) is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Koyoharu Gotouge. It is set in Taisho-era Japan and tells the story of Tanjiro Kamado, a kind-hearted boy who becomes a demon slayer after his family is slaughtered by demons.

The only survivor is his younger sister Nezuko, who has been turned into a demon but surprisingly retains some of her humanity. Tanjiro joins the Demon Slayer Corps to find a cure for Nezuko and avenge his family by hunting down the demon responsible for their deaths.

Story Summary

Tanjiro's journey begins when he returns home to find his entire family murdered by demons, except for Nezuko who has been transformed. He trains under Sakonji Urokodaki to become a demon slayer, mastering Water Breathing techniques. Along his journey, he meets allies like Zenitsu Agatsuma and Inosuke Hashibira, and faces powerful demons including the Twelve Kizuki and their master, Muzan Kibutsuji.
